Introduction to the ATC Exam

The Advanced Titration Certificate (ATC) exam, offered by the Board of Registered Polysomnographic Technologists (BRPT), is designed to recognize advanced knowledge and clinical decision-making in positive airway pressure (PAP) titration and sleep-disordered breathing management.

The ATC is an assessment-based certificate, not a professional credential. Individuals who pass the exam earn a digital badge that can be used for professional recognition on platforms such as LinkedIn and resumes.

The certificate and digital badge are valid for 5 years, after which the exam must be retaken to maintain recognition.
