Domain 5 – Troubleshooting (ATC Exam)

Part of the Advanced Titration Certificate (ATC) exam by BRPT — a digital badge (not a credential) valid for 5 years.

Key Topics

  • Mask leak vs mouth leak identification
  • Pressure intolerance and patient comfort issues
  • Rise time adjustments and synchronization
  • Backup rate and ventilation support issues
  • Improving patient compliance and adherence

Quick Notes

Troubleshooting focuses on identifying barriers to effective therapy, including equipment issues, patient discomfort, and ineffective pressure settings.

Proper adjustments can significantly improve patient tolerance, adherence, and overall treatment outcomes.
