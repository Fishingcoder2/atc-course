Domain 3 – Titration Protocols (ATC Exam)

Part of the Advanced Titration Certificate (ATC) exam by BRPT — a digital badge (not a credential) valid for 5 years.

Key Topics

  • Split-night titration studies
  • CPAP and BiPAP pressure adjustment protocols
  • Recognition and management of treatment-emergent central apneas
  • Maximum pressure guidelines
  • Transitioning between CPAP, BiPAP, and ASV

Quick Notes

Titration protocols focus on optimizing therapy by adjusting pressures to eliminate respiratory events while maintaining patient comfort.

Recognizing patterns such as central apneas, hypoventilation, and persistent hypoxemia is critical in determining when to adjust therapy or change modes.
