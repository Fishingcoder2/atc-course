Sleep Pathways Guild

Sleep Pathways Guild is an independent project focused on organizing study resources, notes, and guided learning pathways in sleep medicine.

The content is designed primarily for sleep technologists, with a strong emphasis on the registered polysomnographic technologist (RPSGT) role.

It also welcomes learners from related backgrounds, including CPSGT, CCSH, respiratory therapists with sleep disorders specialty (RRT-SDS), and EEG technologists with sleep technology experience.

The goal is to help learners navigate both foundational concepts and the changing landscape of sleep technology, including new diagnostics, emerging treatments, and ongoing research.

Purpose of the Guild

This site is intended to grow over time as a structured place for learning, review, and organization in sleep medicine.

  • Organize study resources and learning pathways
  • Support review of core sleep technology concepts
  • Make room for new diagnostics, treatments, and research
  • Provide a clear entry point for focused study projects

First Featured Project

The first major project under Sleep Pathways Guild is a guide for the Advanced Titration Certificate by the Board of Registered Polysomnographic Technologists (BRPT).
