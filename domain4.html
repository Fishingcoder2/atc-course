Domain 4 – Special Populations (ATC Exam)

Part of the Advanced Titration Certificate (ATC) exam by BRPT — a digital badge (not a credential) valid for 5 years.

Key Topics

  • Pediatric titration considerations
  • Neuromuscular disorders and ventilatory support
  • Congestive heart failure and Cheyne-Stokes respiration
  • Obesity hypoventilation syndrome (OHS)
  • Congenital central hypoventilation syndrome

Quick Notes

Special populations require individualized titration approaches due to differences in physiology, disease processes, and tolerance to therapy.

Understanding how conditions such as heart failure, neuromuscular disease, and pediatric status affect breathing patterns is essential for proper PAP management.
